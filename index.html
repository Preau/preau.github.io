About

I'm a 25-year-old developer from 's-Hertogenbosch, the Netherlands. I'm currently employed at Interactive Studios where I create mobile apps, online software and websites. I love learning about new programming languages, frameworks and tools, I also try to keep up-to-date with the ones I already know.

I really love listening to music, and spend a lot of time trying to discover new artists. I also visit music festivals and parties regularly. Other interests include a lot of technology-related things, drinking and trying out craft beers and consuming all kinds of media. Recently I've taken up running, which I'm enjoying a lot so far.
